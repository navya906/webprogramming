Internal Assessment Eligibility

Student Eligibility Rules

To be eligible for the internal assessment, students must meet the following conditions:
attendance must be greater than 75%,
the number of assignments submitted must be less than 5,
and the internal score must be less than or equal to 40.
Specifically, attendance > 75%,
assignment count < 5,
and internal score ≤ 40.
