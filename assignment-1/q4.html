Text Formatting Examples

The bold tag makes text appear in a heavier weight without adding semantic meaning.

The italic tag slants the text to the right, commonly used for emphasis or citations.

The underline tag adds a line beneath the text to highlight important information.

The emphasis tag adds semantic emphasis to text and typically displays in italic format.

The strong tag indicates strong importance and typically displays in bold format with semantic meaning.
